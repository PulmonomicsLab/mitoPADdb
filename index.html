The mitoPADdb is a manually curated database of mitochondrial proteins linked with diseases. It contains the information on mutations and expressional variations of mitochondrial proteins at transcript and protein levels. Here, mitochondrial proteins associated diseases were broadly grouped intoÂ 14 categories. It allows users to access the data using the search option with gene symbol or UniProt accession and also by browse options. The search option provides the information about mutations, expressional variation (both mRNA and protein level) with experimental samples and techniques, and associated diseases with source identifier. The browse option allows users to access the data in three ways: 1) Browse by diseases, 2) Browse by disease-wise expression, and 3) Browse by disease-category pairwise expression. It is a comprehensive mitochondrial proteins database with reference to diseases.
