Mitochondrial Proteins Linked with Diseases (MPLD) is a manually curated database, which provides information about amino acid change due to single nucleotide polymorphisms (SNP) and expressional variation (both mRNA and protein level) of mitochondrial proteins associated with diseases. In this database, mitochondrial diseases have been broadly grouped into neuronal disease, common cancer, heart disease, kidney disease, liver disease, and lung disease. It provides users to access the data using search and browse options.
